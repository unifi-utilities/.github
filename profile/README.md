## unifi-utilities

Since the project has grown far beyond just me, I have determined it is in the project's best interest to no longer be singularly associated with my name. There have been substantial contributions by many. I am grateful for all the help and support I have received, and look forward to seeing the project continue to grow and flourish in its new home.

## Repositories

[unifi-common](https://github.com/unifi-utilities/unifi-common) is the repository for the on_boot.d script.

[unifi-common-addons](https://github.com/unifi-utilities/unifi-common-addons) is the repository for the addons.

[unifios-utilities-archived](https://github.com/unifi-utilities/unifios-utilities-archived) is the repository for the archived utilities.

## Rules

This project was created in peace and love, is free, and will always remain free. Let's keep that spirit alive.

1. Please treat everyone in the community as an equal.
2. People from all skill levels and backgrounds find use of this project, and it is our goal to mentor and grow people's skills.
3. Constructive criticism is encouraged; all other types are not.
4. Please take time to consider how you communicate with others.

## Words of Wisdom

Everyone on the Ubiquiti Discord told me this project was a stupid, useless idea. Nobody would use it in this way. Have the courage of your convictions and follow through! If you find something useful, chances are another will as well!
